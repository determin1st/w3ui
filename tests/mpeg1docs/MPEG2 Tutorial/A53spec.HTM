ATSC Doc A/53 Constraints on MPEG

This section indicates particular constraints or extensions of MPEG documents stated in the body of ATSC Digital Television Standard, Document A/53.  

CAUTION:  This is not an exhaustive list, but gives some particulars that relate to video coding.

Note: complete ATSC documents are available for free on the internet at http:// www.atsc.org.

Subtopics cover constraints stated in the addenda to A/53:

  • Annex A - Video Constraints

  • Annex B - Audio System

  • Annex C - Multiplex and Transport

  • Annex D - RF/Transmission System
