Zig-Zag Scanning Patterns

The zig-zag scanning pattern for run-length coding of the quantized DCT coefficients was established in the original MPEG standard. The same pattern is used for luminance and for chrominance. A modified pattern more suitable for coding of some interlaced picture blocks was added in the MPEG-2 standard.

 

The patterns are represented by the tables below, in which the upper left corner is the DC term.  The tables are defined as

scan[alternate_scan][v][u]

where alternate_scan is a 1-bit flag specifying which pattern to use, v is the index of increasing frequency in the y (vertical) direction, and u is the index of increasing frequency in the x (horizontal) direction.
