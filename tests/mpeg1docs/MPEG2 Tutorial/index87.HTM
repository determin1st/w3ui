MPEG Standards

MPEG standards that are basic to this tutorial and are incorporated into ATSC standards by reference include:

 

ISO/IEC 13818-1

Information technology - Generic coding of moving pictures and associated audio information: Systems

 

ISO/IEC 13818-2

Information technology - Generic coding of moving pictures and associated audio information: Video

The MPEG home page is http://drogo.cselt.stet.it/mpeg/
